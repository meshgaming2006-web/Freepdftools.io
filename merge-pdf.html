How to Combine PDF Files Online for Free

Follow these simple steps to merge your PDFs:

  1. Click the "browse" button or drag and drop your PDF files into the box above.
  2. Arrange the files in your desired order (if needed).
  3. Click the "Merge PDFs" button.
  4. Download your new, combined PDF file.
